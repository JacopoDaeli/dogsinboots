Story

We are a passionate indie game studio based in New York City.

Our small, close-knit team of dedicated engineers, artists, and storytellers is committed to creating engaging and unique games that captivate players and inspire imaginations.

Founded in 2024, our studio began with a simple yet ambitious goal: to craft immersive experiences that push the boundaries of traditional gaming. As we embark on this exciting journey, we are focused on developing our debut game, which promises to showcase our innovative spirit and creative vision.

Despite our small size, our enthusiasm and dedication to excellence drive us forward. We have cultivated a creative environment where innovation thrives, and every team member's voice is valued in the pursuit of excellence.

Our mission is to create worlds that players can get lost in and return to time and time again. We are driven by a love for storytelling and interactive design, continually seeking new ways to engage with our community and enhance the player experience. From intricate narratives and stunning visuals to innovative gameplay mechanics, we are committed to delivering fresh and memorable experiences.

Join us as we explore the endless possibilities of gaming, pushing the limits of what can be achieved in this dynamic and ever-evolving medium. Together, we aim to make our mark on the gaming industry, one game at a time.
